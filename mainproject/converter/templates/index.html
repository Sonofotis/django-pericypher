PeriCypher


PeriCypher uses the periodic table of elements to create a substitution cypher.
Hide your messages in a cypher!

Encryption consists in substituting letters (or group of letters) representing a chemical symbol by its corresponding atomic number.
(Hydrogen H = 1, Helium He = 2, etc.)

If a letter does not exist in the periodic table of elements it will find a letter that exists to the right or left of such letter and then point left or right with > or <
(e.g. K = 19, L = 19>)
